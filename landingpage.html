FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase a painting from your website ?
To purchase a painting, please click on the "Get Quote" button on the painting's page and fill out the form that appears. Our team will review your request and get in touch with you to discuss pricing and shipping options.
How long will it take to receive my painting?
The time it takes to receive your painting will depend on a number of factors, including the size and complexity of the painting, your location, and our current workload. Once you've submitted your quote request, our team will provide you with an estimated delivery date.
How can I purchase a painting from your website?
Shipping costs will vary depending on the size and weight of the painting, as well as your location. Our team will provide you with an accurate shipping quote as part of the pricing discussion after you've submitted your quote request.
